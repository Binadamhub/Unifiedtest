About Us

Our Story

Unified Skill and Acquisition Training Center Ltd. was established with the vision of reducing unemployment and empowering individuals through practical skill acquisition. Recognizing the growing need for vocational skills in Nigeria's evolving economy, we set out to create a center of excellence for skill development.

Since our inception, we have trained hundreds of students who have gone on to establish successful businesses or secure gainful employment in various industries. Our hands-on approach to training ensures that graduates are job-ready from day one.

Our Mission

To empower individuals with market-relevant skills through quality vocational training, fostering self-reliance and economic independence.

Our Vision

To become the leading vocational training center in Northern Nigeria, recognized for producing highly skilled professionals who contribute to national development.

Businesses Started

At Unified Skill and Acquisition Training Center, we measure our success by the positive impact our graduates make in their communities. Our training programs are designed to not just teach skills, but to foster entrepreneurship and self-reliance.

Many of our graduates have gone on to start their own businesses, creating employment opportunities for others in their communities. Others have secured well-paying jobs in various industries, significantly improving their standard of living.

We regularly follow up with our alumni to track their progress and provide ongoing support through mentorship and business development advice.
